Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How does crop recommendation work?
Our system uses soil data, climate patterns, and crop yield history to suggest the most suitable crops for your region.
How do I input land details correctly?

To ensure you receive the best crop recommendations from AgriBloom, please provide the following land details accurately:

  • Soil Type – Select one: Alluvial Soil, Black Soil, Red Soil, or Laterite Soil.
  • Land Area – Enter the total size (e.g., 2 acres or 1 hectare).
  • Water Availability – Specify whether your land is fully irrigated, partially irrigated, or rain-fed.
  • Current Season – Choose the growing season: Spring, Summer, Monsoon, or Winter.
  • Local Climate – Mention average temperature and rainfall conditions if known.

Tip: The more accurate your input, the better AgriBloom can guide your farming journey.

Is the AgriBloom platform easy to use for beginners?
Yes, AgriBloom is designed with simplicity in mind. The interface is user-friendly and all features are clearly labeled, making it easy for first-time users to navigate and understand the crop recommendations and guides.
